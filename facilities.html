OUR FACILITIES

KINDERGARTEN (KG I to KG II)

We cater for students from to Kindergarten level. ensuring that learning opportunities are suitably challenging, cross curricular and meaningful. We strive to foster a ‘love of learning’ in our students and equip them with skills and knowledge to realise their true potential.

PRIMARY (GRADE 1 to GRADE 6)

We cater for students from Pre-Nursery to Year 6, across the Primary School, ensuring that learning opportunities are suitably challenging, cross curricular and meaningful. We strive to foster a ‘love of learning’ in our students and equip them with skills and knowledge to realise their true potential.

SECONDARY (GRADE 7 to GRADE 12)

Our Secondary School includes Years 7 – 12, catering to children of average aged 11.6 years to 16.6 years, with Richard Moore as the Head. In Year 9 pupils begin the International GCSE prior to moving on to study their final qualification before graduation.

AIR CONDITIONS & WELL – MAINTAINED CLASS ROOMS

The school is equipped with air-conditioned Class Rooms & required essential material to compete with new era like Table Chairs, White Boards, DVD & LCD Screen for Kindergarten Students and Projectors for Higher Classes.

MULTIPURPOSE HALL

Multipurpose Hall is established to enhance the Students creativity approach where they present their interpersonal skills confidently by participating different curricular activates e.g. Qirat, Quiz, Speech/Debate Competition, Art & Dramas and Science Exhibition.

PLAY GROUNDS

Both wings (Boys & Girls) of the School have courtyard where Students practice various games under the supervision of their respective Head of House during School Hours for final Competition between and outside school Teams.

TRANSPORT

The school provides private transport facilities to students and charges fare less transport fees as compared to other schools. Following are the routes of the coaches:

  • Jubail
  • R. C
  • Jubail SWCC
  • Al-Hamra

Book Shop

SCIENCE LABORATORIES

Sciences are practical subjects, teaching of which cannot be done properly only in theory form. For proper education of sciences, it is necessary to conduct various kinds of experimental works, which are practical in nature. It is in this place that various kinds of practical works are carry out by the students.

  • Computer Lab
  • Physics Lab / Chemistry Lab
  • Biology Lab

LIBRARY

Due to shifting of Pakistan International School, Jubail building many times, library of students is under progress. Soon, Library would be established with latest trend.

CANTEENS

The school has two canteens for its students; one in the Girls’ Section and one in the Boys’ Section. The Management ensures fresh, nourishing, quality food at cheap rates.

COMPUTER LAB

Well Equipped and highly advanced Computer LAB is also present in the school where students can meet the requirement of new era.
