ABOUT PAKISTAN INTERNATIONAL SCHOOL - JUBAIL

Pakistan International School, Jubail (PISJ), License Number Jeem 2, Ministry of Education, KSA, is affiliated to Federal Board Intermediate & Secondary Education, Islamabad, Pakistan. PIS-Jubail formerly known as the Pakistani Embassy School was founded in 1990. The establishment of PISJ is the zenith of the devoted efforts of the Pakistanis of this region, under the patronage of His Excellency, Ambassador of Pakistan and the active cooperation of the Directorates of Schools, Dammam and Riyadh.

For more than 24 years, PISJ has combined a respect for tradition with an embrace of the future, academic rigor with a caring heart, and an appreciation of a school community. Today, in a world where society is increasingly fragmented and polarized, our school remains a community where honesty and integrity matter, humor and hard work are equally important values, and where the process of education is as important as the grade. We are a school community of high expectations, academic and otherwise.

SECONDARY (GRADE 7 to GRADE 12)
